Akan Cultural Naming

Kwame Nkrumah, the first President of Ghana was names according to the Akan Culture.

In the Akan community found in Ghana - West Africa, newborns are given names basing on the day they weere born and on their gender.

This web application accepts the user's input - birthday date, validates and
output the the child's name depending on the gender selected.
