Akan Cultural Naming

The Akan Community

The Akan naming is derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are named basing on the day they were
born which corresponds to the day of the week and depending on their gender,,
the newborn is named.

In this web application, the user inputs the date of birth of the newborn as per the input fields;
and depending on the gender checked, the application validates and
alerts the Akan name.
